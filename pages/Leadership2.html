AFM Birthday Celebration

On 25 May 2024, we will celebrate the 116th anniversary of the establishment of the Apostolic Faith Mission of South Africa. This significant milestone reminds us of our rich history and the mission entrusted to us. We encourage all assemblies to celebrate this occasion in their celebrations with us for posting on our social media and website.

Pastoral Continuous Development (PCD) Update:

Coming from a racially divided past, and whilst celebrating our God-given diversity, we appreciate our unity as a God-given treasure. We encourage genuine and edifying relationships between believers, pastors, and assemblies. We also encourage spiritual father-son and mentor-mentee relations.
